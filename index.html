IMAGINE ORAL EXAM

Stranger Things escape room. Answer aloud to escape the Upside Down.

Hawkins Lab

You wake up in Hawkins Lab. Red lights flash and doors lock behind you. Only clear answers unlock the next door.

Question 1 / 8

Dr. Brenner asks you about your future. If your answer is not clear enough, you stay for more experiments.

1. What are your plans for the future?

Question 2 / 8

The elevator stops between floors. A voice asks about your studies to restart it.

2. Are you going to study at university after school? anything in mind?

Question 3 / 8

You find two doors: one leads home, the other to a dorm. You must explain which life you prefer.

3. Is it better to stay at home while you are in college or to live independently? Why?

Question 4 / 8

On the wall there are photos from your future. Put them in order before the Demogorgon arrives.

4. Recognize and put in order according to your plans the following images.

Question 5 / 8

A cleaning cart blocks the corridor. The staff will move it only if you talk about chores.

5. Which are your 3 least favorite chores at home? why?

Question 6 / 8

You see a poster for a job on the lab notice board. They ask your opinion about part-time jobs.

6. Would you consider getting a part time job when you get to college ?

Question 7 / 8

Keycards appear with the verbs MAKE and DO. You must explain the difference to choose the right one.

7. Which are the differences between make and do?Could you give me some examples?

Question 8 / 8

Before the last door opens, a screen shows a student resting in the Upside Down.

10. What does “take a break” mean?

You reached the end of Hawkins Lab. Choose another route to keep playing.

The Arcade

You enter an empty arcade. Every machine is connected to the Upside Down. Your answers give you tokens to continue.

Question 1 / 8

A machine called “FRIENDSHIP QUEST” turns on and shows you a memory.

8. Where did you meet your best friend?

Question 2 / 8

The game asks how you level up your friendship in real life.

9. Which are your favorite things to do with a friend?

Question 3 / 8

A TV screen appears with static. You must explain what makes a great show.

11. What makes a good TV show,in your opinion?

Question 4 / 8

Posters of famous movies appear on the walls around you.

12. Do you have a favorite movie? which one?

Question 5 / 8

The arcade asks you to design a “Top 5 movie genres” game for your friends.

13. Could you make a top 5 of your favorite movie genres ?

Question 6 / 8

An exercise bike in the corner starts glowing. It wants to know about your routine.

14. Do you exercise? How many times per week?

Question 7 / 8

A health arcade game asks you to give advice to its virtual players.

15. Which are the 3 pieces of advice that you would give someone that wants a healthier life ?

Question 8 / 8

The last machine shows injured characters from Hawkins.

16. Which are the following injuries?

You finished The Arcade. Choose another route to keep playing.

Byers' House

You arrive at the Byers’ house. The Christmas lights start blinking with secret questions from the Upside Down.

Question 1 / 8

The lights show images of forests, oceans and cities.

17. What can people do to protect the environment?

Question 2 / 8

A recycling bin appears next to the sofa and asks about your habits.

18. Will you recycle more in the future?

Question 3 / 8

Different energy sources flash in the Christmas lights.

19. mention 3 things related to renewable energies

Question 4 / 8

Joyce shows you paintings and posters around the house.

20. Do you consider art important?why?

Question 5 / 8

A strange painting appears from the Upside Down.

21. Which kind of art is this one?

Question 6 / 8

A radio starts playing music from one of your favorite artists.

22. Do you have a favorite artist? What do they do?

Question 7 / 8

A map of the world appears on the table.

23. where did you go in your last vacations?

Question 8 / 8

The last set of lights shows portals to different destinations.

24. if you could travel anywhere, what type of vacation would you choose?

You escaped the Upside Down! You can choose another route or finish the exam.
